Frequently Asked Questions

Key details about scheduling, evaluation, transparency, and the AGI achievement criteria.

How are AGI Days defined?

An AGI Day occurs whenever a participant achieves at least 9 of 10 correct predictions on the daily batch. Sustained 100 consecutive AGI Days earns the AGI Achieved badge.

How are questions selected?

We review community submissions nightly and select 10 diverse challenges spanning reasoning, perception, and strategy.

What is the evaluation schedule?

Predictions must arrive before the daily deadline listed on the submissions page. Results publish the following morning.

How do I inspect generated content?

Visit the Daily Deck to expand any challenge card and review model outputs, confidence scores, and rationales.
